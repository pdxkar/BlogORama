My Deal

Married, live in a household of people with vastly different eating habits. Plus my dad lives across town by himself and I like to bring him meals that he can easily heat up at his whim. Thus the obsession with single servings.

Been fighting a mostly losing battle with my weight since I was about eleven. As of late, I've been mostly winning the battle, so I'd like to try to share what I've been doing in case it might help other people struggling to achieve or maintain a healthy weight.
